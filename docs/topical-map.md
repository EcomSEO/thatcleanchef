# ThatCleanChef — Topical Map

5 hubs × ~30 posts = 150 total. Wave 1 = 40 recipes + 5 diet pillars. Recipe schema on every recipe, Nutrition Ledger on every recipe, original photography on every recipe.

**Keyword data:** DataForSEO April 2026 via `docs/thatcleanchef-research.md` §4.

**Strategic note:** This is a recipe site. The topical map is an Ingredient × Diet × Meal-type matrix. Many recipes cross multiple categories (a cottage cheese flatbread is High-Protein + Mediterranean-adjacent + Breakfast). Tagging handles the multi-dimensional classification; hubs handle the primary organizational structure.

## Strategic notes

**Photography is the primary cost at launch.** 40 recipes × 4 photos each = 160 original images. Batch in 2-day studio shoots of 10 recipes each = 4 shoots total. Budget: $8-15k at launch for food photography (photographer + stylist + prop budget).

**Pinterest is the primary distribution channel.** Every recipe gets a 1000×1500 Pinterest-tall image. 3-5 pin variants per recipe = ~150 pins live by end of Wave 1. Weekly pin scheduling via Tailwind or native Pinterest.

**Trending terms are the month 1-3 traffic goldmine.** Cottage cheese (+240% YoY), cottage cheese flatbread (+620%), protein ice cream (+190%), protein rice krispies (+410%). These rank fast because the search intent is exploding faster than competitors can publish.

**Recipe schema is non-negotiable.** Google's recipe SERP features (rich cards, image, rating stars) depend on complete Recipe schema. Every recipe ships with full schema or doesn't ship.

**Cross-tagging is the organizational advantage.** Cottage Cheese Flatbread is tagged: High-Protein, Mediterranean-adjacent, Breakfast, 30-minute, Vegetarian. One recipe, 5 discovery paths.
